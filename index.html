App Usage Guide

Learn how to get the most out of our Weather Forecast App with this simple guide. Discover all the features and how to use them effectively for your daily planning.

1

Search for Locations

Use the search bar at the top of the app to find weather information for any city worldwide. You can search by city name, postal code, or even coordinates.

2

Understand Weather Cards

Each card displays different weather parameters. The temperature card shows current, min and max temperatures. The humidity card shows moisture levels and "feels like" temperature. The wind card displays speed and direction.

3

Compare Locations

Use the comparison table to see weather conditions in multiple cities at once. This is helpful for travel planning or comparing conditions in different regions.

4

Navigate the App

Use the navigation buttons at the top to switch between the Home view, About information, and this Usage Guide.
